Mission President - Mitchell D. McCuistion

Mitchell Dean McCuistion, 56, and Pamela Hakes McCuistion, six children, Timpview 8th Ward, Orem Utah Timpview Stake: Kansas Wichita Mission, succeeding President Michael L. Bell and Sister Connie Bell.

Brother McCuistion is a former bishop, high councilor, stake Young Men president, Gospel Doctrine teacher, and missionary in the Scotland Edinburgh Mission. Owner, Mitchell Dean LLC. Born in San Bernardino, California, to Horace David McCuistion and Ruth Irene Wells McCuistion. Sister McCuistion serves as a ward Primary presidency counselor and is a former ward Relief Society, Young Women, and Primary president, ward Relief Society and Young Women presidency counselor, and Sunday School teacher. Born in Florence, Arizona, to Clayton H. Hakes and Kathleen Turley Hakes.
