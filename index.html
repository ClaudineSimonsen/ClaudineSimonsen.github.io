Sister Katelyn Simonsen ♥ Kansas Wichita Mission

The Call to the Kansas Wichita Mission

On March 31, 2017 Katelyn opened her mission papers to find out she had been called to serve in the Kansas Wichita Mission.

Dear Sister Simonsen:

You are hereby called to serve as a missionary of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. You are assigned to labor in the Kansas Wichita Mission. It is anticipated you will serve for a period of 18 months.

You should report to the Provo Missionary Training Center on Wednesday, July 5, 2017. You will prepare to preach the gospel in the English language. ...

"Clicking my red shoes, Kansas is going to be my new home for the next 18 months! I've been called to serve in the Wichita Kansas Mission! Leaving July 5th! I can't tell you guys how excited I am to serve the Lord as a missionary!"

Called of God

“I am called of God. My authority is above that of the kings of the earth. By revelation I have been selected as a personal representative of the Lord Jesus Christ. He is my Master and he has chosen me to represent him. To stand in his place, to say and do what he himself would say and do if he personally were ministering to the very people to whom he has sent me. My voice is his voice, and my acts are his acts; my words are his words and my doctrine is his doctrine. My commission is to do what he wants done. To say what he wants said. To be a living modern witness in word and deed of the divinity of his great and marvelous latter-day work.”   Bruce R. McConkie
